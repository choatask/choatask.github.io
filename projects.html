Bookclass
About

Bookclass is a website for booking classrooms, canceling classrooms, and viewing class schedules. There are two roles: admin and user. The admin role can book classrooms, cancel classrooms, and view the history of booking classrooms. The user role can view the class schedule. Classrooms that have been booked will turn blue, and when the space is hovered over, the classroom information will appear.
